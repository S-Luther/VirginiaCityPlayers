Interacting with Tickets

To view, add, delete, or edit tickets simply click the Enter Tickets button on a shows specific tab on the Shows page.

Printing Tickets

To Print a Ticket press the Enter Tickets button on the show you desire. Then press the print button on the ticket you'd like to print.
